* **Vibe:** University Professor / Wizard.

* **Watch:** His "Crust of Rust" series is legendary for understanding Lifetimes and Iterators.

## 4. The Primeagen
* **Vibe:** Chaotic, loud, Netflix Engineer.
* **Content:** Entertaining rants about Rust vs Go, and very good content about Rust tooling/Vim.
